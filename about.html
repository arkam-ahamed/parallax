My Info


Born and raised in Sri Lanka, I had the opportunity to venture into the tropical jungles to observe wildlife at close quarters.I currently reside in Balham, London. My home is also my studio, which I have set up in a way to get the most natural light.

Interests

  • Bird Watching from a very young age
  • Wild life Photography during travel
  • Travelling and around SriLanka England
  • Art and painting of various animals

Achievements

  • Committee Member of the London Bird Club (part of the LNHS)
  • Photograph I took adorns the back cover of the London Bird Atlas
  • Studied in Vaibhav school of fine arts
  • Member of LSB and RSPB member groups

Current Life

All the reference material for my wildlife paintings are from various excursions around Sri Lanka and England, with a handful of shot in other countries. I'm Committee Member of the London Bird Club (part of the LNHS), and I regularly travel with the LBC and RSPB member groups all around the UK to do my part in the conservation of fauna and flora.
